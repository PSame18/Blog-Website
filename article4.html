Advantages of reading books:

  1. Increases verbal/writing skills
  2. Helps articulate thoughts
  3. Enhances problem solving skills
  4. Increases concentration
  5. Improves memory
  6. Improves the relaxation response, lowering heartbeat and pulse rate
